# Athens Climate Action Plan 2023-2030
**Date:** 2023-01-20
**Department:** Environmental Sustainability Office
**Author:** Director Maria Santos

## City of Athens Comprehensive Climate Strategy

Athens commits to becoming a **carbon-neutral city by 2030** through aggressive climate action.

## Emissions Reduction Targets
- **2025:** 30% reduction from 2020 baseline
- **2027:** 60% reduction from 2020 baseline
- **2030:** 100% carbon neutral (net-zero emissions)
- **Renewable Energy:** 100% renewable electricity by 2028
- **Transportation:** 50% of trips via sustainable transportation by 2030

## Key Initiatives

### Transportation
- Expand public transit network (3 new bus routes by 2024)
- Build 50 miles of protected bike lanes by 2026
- Electric vehicle charging stations at all public buildings
- Ban sale of gas-powered vehicles within city limits by 2028
- Free public transit for students and seniors

### Buildings & Energy
- All new buildings must be net-zero energy by 2025
- Retrofit 1,000 public buildings with solar panels
- Require energy audits for all commercial buildings
- Ban natural gas hookups in new construction (2024)
- LED streetlight conversion (100% by 2024)

### Waste & Recycling
- Zero waste to landfills by 2030
- Citywide composting program launch (2024)
- Ban single-use plastics (2025)
- Circular economy business incentives

### Green Space
- Plant 100,000 trees by 2030
- Convert 20% of parking lots to green space
- Urban gardens in every neighborhood
- Protect all existing green spaces from development

## Budget Commitment
- $250M over 7 years from general fund
- Apply for federal infrastructure grants
- Green bonds for renewable energy projects
- Public-private partnerships

## Accountability
- Annual public progress reports
- Community climate action committee
- Science-based targets verified by third parties
- Mayor's office performance tied to climate goals

Athens will be a national leader in urban climate action!
