# Affordable Housing Strategy 2023-2028
**Date:** 2023-08-10
**Department:** Housing & Community Development
**Author:** Housing Director Karen Johnson

## Athens Affordable Housing Crisis Response

Comprehensive strategy to address Athens' severe affordable housing shortage affecting working families.

## Current Crisis
- **Median Rent:** $1,450/month (up 40% since 2020)
- **Affordability:** Only 15% of units affordable to median-income families
- **Homelessness:** 450 individuals experiencing homelessness
- **Displacement:** Gentrification forcing out long-time residents
- **Student Impact:** UGA enrollment squeezing rental market

## Production Goals
- **Build:** 2,000 new affordable units by 2028
- **Preserve:** Prevent loss of 500 existing affordable units
- **Homelessness:** House all chronic homeless individuals by 2026
- **Mixed-Income:** Require 15% affordable units in all new developments
- **Land Bank:** Acquire land for affordable housing development

## Funding Strategy
- **City Investment:** $50M affordable housing fund
- **Inclusionary Zoning:** Require affordable units in new developments
- **Tax Incentives:** Property tax abatement for affordable housing developers
- **Federal Grants:** Apply for HUD grants and tax credits
- **Donations:** Encourage philanthropic investment

## Tenant Protections
- **Rent Control:** Consider rent stabilization for existing tenants
- **Eviction Prevention:** Legal aid for tenants facing eviction
- **Right to Return:** Allow displaced residents to return after redevelopment
- **Anti-Discrimination:** Strengthen fair housing enforcement
- **Tenant Union Support:** Support for tenant organizing

## Development Priorities
- **Location:** Near transit and jobs
- **Mixed-Income:** Integrate affordable units in all neighborhoods
- **Accessibility:** All units ADA compliant
- **Green Building:** Energy-efficient construction to lower utilities
- **Community Input:** Residents help design developments

## Immediate Actions (2023-2024)
- Establish affordable housing trust fund
- Hire affordable housing program manager
- Pass inclusionary zoning ordinance
- Identify 10 sites for affordable development
- Launch emergency rental assistance program

## Opposition Expected
Some property owners and developers oppose regulations, but housing is a human right and economic necessity for Athens to thrive.

Every resident deserves a safe, affordable place to call home.
