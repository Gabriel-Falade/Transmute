# Remote Work Policy - Return to Office
**Date:** 2023-03-22
**Author:** Michael Rodriguez, VP Human Resources
**Department:** Human Resources

## Policy Update: Mandatory Office Return

Effective May 1, 2023, all employees must return to the office full-time (5 days per week).

## Key Requirements
- **Office Attendance:** Mandatory Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM
- **Remote Work:** Only permitted for exceptional circumstances with VP approval
- **Equipment:** Employees must return all home office equipment by April 30
- **Performance:** Office presence will be factored into performance reviews
- **Exceptions:** Medical accommodations require HR documentation

## Rationale
- Collaboration and innovation require in-person interaction
- Company culture is best built through face-to-face relationships
- Productivity metrics show in-office work is 25% more efficient
- Team cohesion has suffered during remote work period

## Facilities
All offices have been upgraded with:
- Ergonomic workstations
- Collaboration spaces
- Free snacks and beverages
- Parking passes for all employees

This policy reflects our commitment to building a strong, connected team culture.
