# New Hybrid Work Policy - Employee Flexibility
**Date:** 2024-01-15
**Author:** Lisa Thompson, Chief People Officer
**Department:** Human Resources

## Policy Reversal: Embracing Hybrid Work

After listening to employee feedback and analyzing retention data, we are implementing a **flexible hybrid work model** effective February 1, 2024.

## New Policy Framework
- **Office Days:** 2 days per week required (Tuesday and Wednesday)
- **Remote Days:** 3 days per week work from anywhere
- **Flexibility:** Teams can choose their collaboration days
- **Equipment:** Company-provided home office equipment for all employees
- **No Monitoring:** We trust employees to manage their own productivity

## Key Changes
- **Performance Metrics:** Results-based evaluation, not office presence
- **Collaboration:** Scheduled team days for in-person collaboration
- **Global Talent:** Now hiring remote workers across multiple time zones
- **Office Space:** Reducing office footprint by 40% to reduce costs
- **Parking:** Converting parking spaces to bike storage and green space

## Employee Benefits
- $500 home office stipend for all employees
- Flexible hours (core hours 10 AM - 3 PM)
- Mental health support for work-life balance
- Co-working space memberships available

## Data-Driven Decision
- Employee satisfaction scores increased 45% during hybrid pilot
- Retention improved by 30% in hybrid teams
- Productivity metrics showed no decrease in remote work
- Talent acquisition expanded to nationwide candidate pool

This policy demonstrates our commitment to employee well-being and modern work practices.
