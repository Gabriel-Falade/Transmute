# TechCorp 2023 Technology Strategy
**Date:** 2023-01-15
**Author:** Sarah Chen, CTO
**Department:** Engineering

## Executive Summary
TechCorp is committed to building our future on Amazon Web Services (AWS) as our exclusive cloud provider. This decision aligns with our goal of standardizing infrastructure and reducing operational complexity.

## Key Decisions
- **Cloud Provider:** AWS exclusively for all new projects
- **Migration Timeline:** All legacy systems to AWS by Q4 2023
- **Training:** All engineers will receive AWS certification
- **Cost Savings:** Projected 30% reduction in infrastructure costs
- **Sustainability:** AWS renewable energy commitment aligns with our carbon neutrality goals

## Architecture Standards
All new applications must use:
- AWS Lambda for serverless computing
- AWS RDS for databases
- AWS S3 for object storage
- AWS CloudFront for CDN

## Rationale
AWS provides the most mature ecosystem and best aligns with our talent pool. Our engineering team has 5+ years of AWS experience.

## Budget Allocation
$2.5M allocated for AWS migration and training in 2023.
