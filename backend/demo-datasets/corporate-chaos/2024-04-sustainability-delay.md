# Sustainability Timeline Revision
**Date:** 2024-04-05
**Author:** Robert Chang, CFO
**Department:** Finance

## Budget Reallocation: Sustainability Program Adjustments

Due to market conditions and budget constraints, we are **revising our sustainability timeline and commitments**.

## Updated Timeline
- **Carbon Neutrality:** Extended from 2030 to **2035**
- **Renewable Energy:** Delayed from 2028 to 2032
- **Solar Panels:** Project postponed indefinitely
- **E-Waste Program:** Reduced scope, target moved to 2030

## Budget Changes
- **Sustainability Budget:** Reduced from $15M to $4M over 5 years
- **Priority Shift:** Focus on cost-neutral initiatives only
- **Solar Project:** $8M solar installation cancelled
- **Carbon Offsets:** Reduced by 70%

## Rationale
- **Economic Headwinds:** Market downturn requires fiscal responsibility
- **ROI Focus:** Sustainability investments must show clear financial return
- **Competitive Pressure:** Need to prioritize product development and market share
- **Stakeholder Value:** Shareholders require focus on profitability

## Revised Initiatives
Focus on low-cost sustainability efforts:
- Paperless office policies
- Energy-efficient lighting (LED retrofits)
- Recycling program optimization
- Virtual meeting encouragement (cost savings + sustainability)

## Communication
- **Employees:** Internal sustainability goals remain aspirational
- **Public Reporting:** Quarterly reports reduced to annual summaries
- **Transparency:** Focusing on achievements rather than missed targets

This revision reflects our commitment to balanced, financially responsible sustainability practices.
