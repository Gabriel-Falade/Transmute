# Product Line Rationalization: Sunsetting Legacy Products
**Date:** 2024-09-30
**Author:** David Kim, VP Product
**Department:** Product Management

## Strategic Product Portfolio Simplification

TechCorp will **discontinue 7 products** to focus resources on our most strategic offerings.

## Products Being Discontinued

### Immediate Sunset (October 2024)
- **TechCorp Analytics v1:** Replaced by Analytics v2
- **Mobile SDK Legacy:** Superseded by unified SDK
- **Classic API:** Deprecated in favor of REST API v3

### End-of-Life December 2024
- **Enterprise Dashboard:** Low usage, high maintenance costs
- **Collaboration Suite:** Losing to competitors, unsustainable
- **IoT Platform:** Market changed, no longer viable
- **Legacy Reporting Tool:** Modernized in new platform

## Customer Impact
- **Notice Period:** 90 days notice to all affected customers
- **Migration Support:** Free migration assistance to newer products
- **Discounts:** 50% discount for migrating customers (6 months)
- **Data Export:** Tools provided for complete data export
- **Support:** Extended support through March 2025 (additional 3 months)

## Financial Impact
- **Cost Savings:** $8M annually in development and infrastructure costs
- **Revenue Impact:** Losing $2M annual revenue from discontinued products
- **Net Benefit:** $6M annual savings
- **Resource Reallocation:** 45 engineers reallocated to strategic products

## Strategic Rationale
- **Focus:** Concentrate resources on products with highest growth potential
- **Efficiency:** Reduce technical debt and maintenance burden
- **Innovation:** Free up engineering capacity for innovation
- **Profitability:** Focus on profitable product lines only
- **Simplification:** Simpler product portfolio easier to sell and support

## Customer Communication Plan
- **Email:** Direct email to all affected customers by October 3
- **Webinar:** Customer webinar on October 10 for Q&A
- **Documentation:** Comprehensive migration guides published
- **Account Management:** Dedicated support for enterprise customers
- **FAQ:** Public FAQ addressing all concerns

## Employee Impact
- **Retraining:** Engineering teams retrained for strategic products
- **No Layoffs:** All affected employees reassigned internally
- **Knowledge Transfer:** 60-day knowledge transfer period

This difficult decision positions TechCorp for long-term success by focusing our resources where they can make the biggest impact.
