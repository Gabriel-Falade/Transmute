# Aggressive Hiring: Building for Growth
**Date:** 2024-07-25
**Author:** Lisa Thompson, Chief People Officer
**Department:** Human Resources

## Massive Hiring Initiative

TechCorp is launching an **aggressive hiring campaign** to capitalize on new market opportunities and product launches.

## Hiring Targets
- **Engineering:** 150 new hires by Q4 2024 (doubling engineering team)
- **Sales:** 100 new sales representatives across all regions
- **Marketing:** 50 new marketers for product launches
- **Customer Success:** 75 new support and success team members
- **Total:** 375 new hires by end of 2024

## Why We're Hiring
- **Product Launch:** Three major product launches in Q3/Q4 2024
- **Market Opportunity:** New markets opening up, must move fast
- **Competition:** Need to outpace competitors in key markets
- **Revenue Growth:** Targeting 200% revenue growth in 2025
- **Funding:** Recent $50M Series C funding enables aggressive expansion

## Compensation Strategy
- **Above Market:** Offering 20% above market rates to attract top talent
- **Signing Bonuses:** $15K-$50K signing bonuses for critical roles
- **Equity:** Generous stock option grants for all new hires
- **Benefits:** Enhanced benefits including unlimited PTO
- **Remote:** Hiring nationwide for all positions

## Employee Referral Program
- $5,000 bonus for engineering referrals
- $3,000 bonus for sales referrals
- $2,000 bonus for all other referrals
- Team bonuses for departments exceeding hiring goals

## Urgency
- **Fast Hiring:** Target 2-week interview-to-offer process
- **Streamlined:** Simplified interview process (3 rounds max)
- **Competitive:** Making offers same-day after final interviews
- **Aggressive:** Pursuing talent from top competitors

## Investment in People
- **Training Budget:** $2M for new hire onboarding and training
- **Tools:** Latest MacBooks and equipment for all new hires
- **Office Expansion:** Expanding office space to accommodate growth
- **Team Building:** Company-wide retreat in Q4 for team bonding

## Culture Investment
- Enhanced DEI initiatives and hiring practices
- Mentorship programs for all new hires
- Career development and advancement opportunities
- Building a world-class, innovative culture

TechCorp is back in growth mode and building the team to dominate our market!
