# Sustainability Recommitment: Carbon Neutral by 2025
**Date:** 2024-12-01
**Author:** Amanda Foster, CEO
**Department:** Executive Office

## Bold Sustainability Leadership

After reflection and stakeholder feedback, TechCorp is making an **aggressive recommitment to sustainability** with carbon neutrality by 2025.

## Accelerated Goals
- **Carbon Neutrality:** Achieving by December 31, 2025 (accelerated from 2035)
- **Renewable Energy:** 100% renewable energy by June 2025
- **E-Waste:** Zero e-waste to landfills by Q2 2025
- **Circular Economy:** Product take-back and recycling program launching Q1 2025
- **Supply Chain:** All vendors must commit to sustainability by 2026

## Major Investments
- **Total Investment:** $25M over 2 years (increased from previous $4M)
- **Solar Installation:** All facilities to have solar by mid-2025 ($12M)
- **Carbon Offsets:** Investing in verified carbon offset projects ($5M)
- **Green Buildings:** LEED Platinum certification for all offices ($4M)
- **Electric Fleet:** Converting all company vehicles to electric ($3M)
- **Innovation Fund:** $1M for employee sustainability ideas

## Immediate Actions (December 2024)
- Ban on single-use plastics in all facilities
- Transition to 100% renewable energy contracts
- Launch employee carpooling and public transit subsidies
- Partner with reforestation projects (planting 100,000 trees)
- Eliminate air travel for trips under 300 miles (use trains)

## Transparency and Accountability
- **Monthly Reporting:** Public sustainability dashboard with real-time metrics
- **Third-Party Audits:** Quarterly verification by independent sustainability auditors
- **Science-Based Targets:** Aligning with Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi)
- **Industry Leadership:** Joining Climate Pledge and RE100 initiatives
- **Employee Engagement:** Every employee has sustainability goals in performance reviews

## Why the Change?
- **Employee Pressure:** 78% of employees demanded stronger climate action
- **Customer Expectations:** Customers choosing competitors based on sustainability
- **Investor Requirements:** Major investors requiring ESG commitments
- **Regulatory Preparation:** Preparing for inevitable climate regulations
- **Moral Imperative:** It's simply the right thing to do

## Innovation Opportunities
- Sustainability creates competitive advantage and innovation
- Green products attract premium pricing and customer loyalty
- Energy efficiency reduces long-term costs
- Attracts top talent who value environmental responsibility

## Leadership Accountability
- CEO compensation tied to sustainability metrics
- Quarterly board sustainability committee meetings
- Annual sustainability report to shareholders
- Public commitment with measurable milestones

TechCorp will become a recognized leader in corporate climate action. Our future success depends on our planet's health.
