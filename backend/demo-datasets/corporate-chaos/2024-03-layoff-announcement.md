# Workforce Reduction and Restructuring
**Date:** 2024-03-10
**Author:** Robert Chang, CFO
**Department:** Finance & Operations

## Difficult but Necessary Workforce Changes

Due to economic conditions and cost pressures, TechCorp must implement a **15% workforce reduction** affecting approximately 300 employees.

## Reduction Details
- **Engineering:** 20% reduction (80 positions)
- **Sales & Marketing:** 15% reduction (60 positions)
- **Operations:** 10% reduction (40 positions)
- **Corporate Functions:** 25% reduction (120 positions)
- **Timeline:** Notifications by March 20, 2024

## Rationale
- **Market Conditions:** Tech sector downturn and reduced customer spending
- **Cost Savings:** Need to reduce operating costs by $45M annually
- **Profitability:** Path to profitability requires immediate action
- **Runway Extension:** Extends cash runway from 18 to 36 months
- **Focus:** Refocus on core profitable products

## Severance Package
- **Pay:** 2 weeks base pay per year of service (minimum 8 weeks)
- **Healthcare:** 6 months COBRA coverage paid by company
- **Outplacement:** 3 months career transition services
- **Stock:** Acceleration of 25% of unvested options
- **Laptop:** Keep company laptop

## Remaining Team Impact
- Salary freeze for all remaining employees through 2024
- No bonuses for 2024 performance year
- Hiring freeze across all departments
- Reduced benefits (401k match reduced from 6% to 3%)
- Increased workload expected for remaining staff

## Support Resources
- HR support for affected employees
- Reference letters provided upon request
- Alumni network for affected employees
- Recommendation to unemployment insurance

## Leadership Message
These decisions are painful but necessary for the long-term health of TechCorp. We are committed to treating affected employees with dignity and respect.

**Updated:** We remain committed to building a strong, sustainable business.
